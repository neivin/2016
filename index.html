About

CUSEC brings together passionate students from across Canada. Meet like-minded peers, learn from world-renowned experts and discover career opportunities with exciting companies.

In 2002, CUSEC was formed by a group of inspired individuals who were dedicated to connecting students that share a passion for software. After 15 years, the CUSEC organizing team have only become stronger. As a student run non-profit organization, CUSEC allows for hundreds of students from all across Canada to come together to talk, learn, and have fun. The team is committed to continuously encouraging and engaging more and more students.
